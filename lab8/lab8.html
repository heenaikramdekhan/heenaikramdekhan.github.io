Halwa Puri

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina (suji)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup ghee or clarified butter
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour (atta)
  • Water for kneading
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

For Halwa:

Heat ghee in a pan and add semolina. Roast on low heat until it turns golden brown and aromatic.

In a separate pan, heat water and sugar. Once the sugar dissolves, add the cardamom powder, saffron strands, and food color.

Slowly add the sugar syrup to the roasted semolina while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.

Cook until the halwa thickens and starts to leave the sides of the pan. Add chopped nuts and mix well.

Remove from heat and keep warm.

Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain.

2. In a skillet, cook pancetta or bacon over medium heat until crispy. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute.

3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and black pepper.

4. Add cooked spaghetti to the skillet with pancetta. Remove from heat and quickly stir in the egg mixture, tossing until the eggs thicken and coat the pasta. If needed, add reserved pasta water to loosen the sauce. Serve immediately.

Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 400g chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant.

2. Add sliced chicken breast and stir-fry until cooked through.

3. Add sliced bell pepper and onion to the skillet and cook until vegetables are tender-crisp.

4. Stir in soy sauce and oyster sauce, and season with salt and black pepper to taste. Cook for another minute.

5. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Caprese Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 large tomatoes, sliced
  • 200g fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

1. Arrange alternating slices of tomato and mozzarella cheese on a serving plate.

2. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices.

3. Drizzle balsamic glaze over the salad.

4. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Serve immediately.

Vegetable Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice, chilled
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant.

2. Push garlic to the side and pour beaten eggs into the skillet. Scramble until cooked, then mix with garlic.

3. Add mixed vegetables to the skillet and stir-fry until tender.

4. Add chilled cooked rice to the skillet, breaking up any clumps with a spatula.

5. Pour soy sauce over the rice and vegetables, and toss to combine. Cook until heated through.

6. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Serve hot.

Mushroom Risotto

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 200g mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup white wine (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

1. In a saucepan, heat vegetable broth over medium heat. Keep it warm but not boiling.

2. In a separate large skillet or pot, melt butter over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, and sautÃ© until softened.

3. Add Arborio rice to the skillet and cook, stirring constantly, until the rice is translucent around the edges.

4. If using white wine, pour it into the skillet and stir until absorbed.

5. Add sliced mushrooms to the skillet and cook until they release their moisture.

6. Begin adding warm vegetable broth to the skillet, one ladleful at a time, stirring constantly and allowing the rice to absorb the liquid before adding more.

7. Continue this process until the rice is creamy and tender, about 20-25 minutes.

8. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese, and season with salt and black pepper to taste. Serve hot.

Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F (175Â°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.

2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.

3. Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in vanilla extract.

4. In a separate bowl, whisk together all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add dry ingredients to the butter mixture and mix until well combined.

5. Fold in semisweet chocolate chips.

6. Drop tablespoonfuls of dough onto prepared baking sheets, leaving space between each cookie.

7. Bake in preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until edges are golden brown.

8. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring to wire racks to cool completely. Enjoy!
