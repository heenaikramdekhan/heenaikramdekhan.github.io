Breakfast Savory Spinach and Feta Omelette
  1. 2 eggs
  2. 1/4 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  3. 2 tablespoons feta cheese, crumbled
  4. Salt and pepper to taste
  5. 1 tablespoon olive oil
  1. In a bowl, beat the eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Add chopped spinach to the pan and sauté for a minute until it wilts.
  3. Pour the beaten eggs over the spinach in the pan.
  4. Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese evenly over the eggs.
  5. Allow the eggs to set slightly around the edges, then use a spatula to lift the edges and let the uncooked egg flow underneath.
  6. Cook for another minute or until the omelette is cooked through.
Lunch Turkey and Avocado Wrap
  1. 1 tortilla
  2. 4-6 slices of turkey breast
  3. 1/2 avocado, sliced
  4. Handful of spinach leaves
  5. Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Lay the tortilla flat on a plate or a clean surface.
  2. Place the turkey slices evenly across the tortilla.
  3. Add the sliced avocado on top of the turkey.
  4. Place a handful of spinach leaves over the turkey and avocado.
  5. If desired, spread a thin layer of mustard or mayo over the ingredients
  6. Sprinkle salt and pepper to taste.
Dinner Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta
  1. 8 oz linguine or spaghett
  2. 1/2 lb medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  3. 3 tablespoons olive oil
  4. 3 cloves garlic, minced
  5. Juice of 1 lemon,Red pepper flakes,Fresh parsley, chopped
  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside..
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat
  3. Add minced garlic and sauté for about 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque.
  5. Stir in lemon zest and lemon juice. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using).
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet, tossing to coat the pasta in the lemon garlic sauce and to combine with the shrimp.
