Penguins



  1. Penguins live in many locations around the Southern Hemisphere
  2. At very quick glance they look alike with white and black bodies, but they all have unique characteristics.
  3. Penguins have several adaptations that help them thrive in cold environments.
  4. Penguins are flightless birds. Instead of wings, they have flippers that help them navigate through water with incredible agility.
